Sweedish brown sauce

metadata

Created on 2020-12-03

Adapted from IKEA MEATBALLS AT HÖME - CREAM SAUCE by IKEA UK @ Twitter (2020-12-03)

Estimated time needed: At least 9 minutes. At most 9 minutes.

Ingredients

300 g water
150 ml double cream approximately 37 % fat
40 g butter
40 g flour
15 g vegetable bouillon
15 g beef bouillon
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp Dijon mustard

Scaling

Percent %
By ingredient

Timeline diagram

Directions

⌛ Active time 2 minutes

  • 40 g butter

Melt butter

⌛ Active time 2 minutes

  • 40 g flour

Add flour and brown:

Whisk in the flour and brown it for 2 minutes (while whisking).

⌛ Active time 1 minutes

  • 15 g vegetable bouillon
  • 15 g beef bouillon
  • 300 g water

Add stock:

While continuing to stir, add vegetable and beef stock. (The original recipe called for 150 ml of beef stock and 150 ml of vegetable stock. However, it's easier for us to scale it when it's given in terms of bouillon, which we can readily obtain in cubes or granulated. The amounts here assume 50 g bouillon (1 cube) per 500 ml water to get stock.)

⌛ Active time 1 minutes

  • 150 ml double cream | approximately 37 % fat
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Add cream and seasoning

⌛ Active time 3 minutes

Bring to simmer and allow to thicken
