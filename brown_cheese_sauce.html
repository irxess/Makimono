Brown cheese sauce

A savory sayce with tones of juniper and sweetness, making it good for dishes based on game meat. Or simply when you'd use a gravy.

Ingredients

  • 300 g cream
  • 1 beef bouillon cubes
  • 3 tbsp game stock
  • 100 g Gudbrandsdalsost
  • 1 tbsp juniper berries
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp corn starch
