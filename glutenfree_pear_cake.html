Gluten-free Pear Cake

This cake is moist and full of air, just like a normal cake, thanks to the eggs.

Ingredients

  • 440 g gluten-free flour
  • 300 g sugar
  • 250 g butter
  • 8 eggs
  • 15 g baking powder
  • 5 g baking soda
  • 750 g pears
  • 2 tbsp vanilla extract
  • other spices

Directions

  • 750 g pears
Dice the pears: Peel the pears and chop them into small cubes.
  • 2 tbsp vanilla extract
  • other spices
Warm the pears: Put the pears into a frying pan, adding vanilla extract and the optional other spices. Warm on medium-low heat until the pears are soft, but not falling apart yet. Set aside to cool
  • 250 g butter
Soften butter: Soften the butter in a microwave oven or a small saucepan.
  • 8 eggs
  • 150 g sugar
Split eggs: Split the eggs. Egg whites should go into a kitchen machine bowl, and the yolks in another bowl. Add half of the sugar to the yolks.
  • 150 g sugar
Beat egg white and sugar: Add the rest of the sugar to the egg whites. Beat the egg whites and sugar stiff.
  • 440 g gluten-free flour
  • 15 g baking powder
  • 5 g baking soda
Mix everything else: Add the dry ingredients to the egg yolks. Also add the water from the pears. Mix together. You can add ab bit more water if it is hard to mix. When the batter is smooth, add the pears.
Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to 160 degrees Celsius.
Fold in pears: Carefully fold the two mixture's together. Try to stop mixing as soon as the batter is an even color. Pour the batter into a deep baking tray.
Bake: Bake the cake for about 45 minutes, until golden-brown on top and a cake skewer comes out clean.
